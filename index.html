About Me

Hello! I'm Nikhil Kant, a Data Science Enthusiast with a strong passion for solving complex problems through technology. Formerly working as a Machine Learning Engineer at Vertiv, I developed data driven solutions to improve Operations, Supply Chain, and Product Development. Being passionate about predictive analytics, process optimization, and data-driven decision-making, I specialize in crafting solutions that empower businesses and systems.

Currently pursuing my M.S. in Information Systems at the University of Maryland with a focus in Business Intelligence and Advanced Analytics. My mission is to bridge the gap between data and actionable insights to drive growth and innovation.
